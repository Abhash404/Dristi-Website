COMMUNITY EYE HEALTH EDUCATION

We are Involved in philanthropic service like carrying out free cataract screening and referral to the poor and needy patients. At the community services we provide clinical eye services including refraction, eye health education, dispensing protective glasses and medicines to needy people. The health promotion and education activities increase eye care and general health awareness in general. Community Eye Care is always exploring the opportunities to serve the community, either through screenings, examinations or education. Community eye works at Tilganga eye hospital is one of the strong activities of the organization and we do participate in this novel works regularly.
