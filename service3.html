OCULOPLASTIC SERVICES

Oculoplastic surgery is the cosmetic, corrective, and reconstructive surgery of the eye. It manages and repairs problems primarily related to the tissues or structures surrounding the eye, rather than the eyeball itself. These structures include the eyelids, the tear ducts, and the orbit. some problems are ptosis, eyelid misposition, tear drainage, skin cancer of face and eye, bulging eyes, anopthalmia, facial paralysis, etc. A wide variety of reconstructive surgical procedures are conducted to improve the function, comfort and appearance of these structures.
